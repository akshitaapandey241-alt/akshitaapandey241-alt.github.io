AP Research · Systematic Review & Meta-Analysis

ACE Subtypes & Alzheimer's Disease Risk

Independent systematic review examining how specific adverse childhood experience subtypes — emotional neglect, physical abuse, sexual abuse, household dysfunction — differentially predict Alzheimer's disease-related outcomes. Screened 159 PubMed records, included 24 studies (n = up to 434,215) spanning the US, UK, China, Japan, and Australia. Key finding: maltreatment-type ACEs, particularly emotional neglect, show stronger and more consistent associations with cognitive decline than household dysfunction — a subtype distinction obscured by cumulative ACE scoring.

Linear Algebra · Quantitative Modeling Project

Modeling NYC Population Equilibrium via Markov Chains

Constructed a 4-zone stochastic transition matrix modeling population movement across the NYC metropolitan area (Manhattan, Inner Ring, Suburbs, Exurbs). Proved λ = 1 is an eigenvalue via RREF, solved for the steady-state eigenvector, and performed a sensitivity analysis modeling the long-run effects of a $500M light rail investment on population distribution. Computed rate of convergence using the second-largest eigenvalue (λ₂ = 0.70), estimating equilibrium in approximately 65 years.
